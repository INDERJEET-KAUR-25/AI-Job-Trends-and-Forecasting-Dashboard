# AI-Job-Trends-and-Forecasting-Dashboard
This is dashboard showing job trends and forecasting data. It has four pages: 1.) Home 2.)Executive Summary 3.) Salary and Experience Analysis 4.) Skills and Role Insights
KEY OBSERVATION

   The analysis highlights four major insights:

 AI employment is increasing each year, showing strong market momentum.
Technical skills—especially Python, Machine Learning, and Data Science—are essential and commonly required.
Salaries vary across job roles, with senior AI and ML positions offering significantly higher compensation.
Specific regions show stronger hiring and higher pay, indicating emerging global talent hubs.


Additionally, cloud and ML skills are becoming

more relevant, signaling a shift from experimentation towards scalable AI implementations. These observations help understand current trends and guide career planning, hiring strategies, and learning priorities 
Summary of Analysis



The dashboard provides a clear picture of how rapidly the AI job market is expanding across industries and countries. Full-time roles dominate hiring, and salaries are increasing for advanced technical positions such as Machine Learning and Data Science. Skill trends show that Python, ML, and cloud technologies are currently most in demand, reflecting the shift towards real-world AI deployment. The forecasting section also indicates steady future growth, suggesting continuous opportunities in the coming years. Overall, the findings confirm that AI remains a fast-growing field with strong career potential and evolving skill requirements.
